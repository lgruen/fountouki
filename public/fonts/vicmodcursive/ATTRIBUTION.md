# Victorian Modern Cursive (unjoined / print)

Bundled fonts:
- `VicModernCursive-Regular.ttf`
- `VicModernCursive-Bold.ttf`

These are the regular and bold weights of the unjoined ("manuscript")
form of the Victorian Modern Cursive typeface — the print-style
letterforms taught in early years of Victorian state schools, used here
as the canonical letter shape across this app (phonics recognition,
and the writing game's tracing references).

## Source

Published by the State of Victoria (Department of Education), available
on the Arc resource library:

- Resource page: https://arc.educationapps.vic.gov.au/learning/resource/80715/handwriting-fonts

The original archive is `vicmodcursive.zip`, which contains
`Vicmorg_.ttf` (regular) and `Vicmob__.ttf` (bold). Files are bundled
here unmodified, renamed only for clarity.

The same archive also contains `VICCURSI.TTF` (the joined "VicCursive"
variant). That file is not bundled — the unjoined print form is the
correct starting point for preschool / early-years writing, and the
joined cursive belongs to a later phase.
